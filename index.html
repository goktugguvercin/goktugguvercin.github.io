Philosophy & Approach

I am a Machine Learning Scientist specializing in Language Modeling, Agentic AI, and Computer Vision. I view these fields not as separate disciplines, but as integral, interconnected components of intelligence to build cognitive thinking systems.

From human perspective, the world is sensed and interpreted through sight, sound, and interaction. We then reason through this multimodal experience, draw some conclusions and compress it into the language to communicate or into the decisions to take some actions. This necessitates multimodality with relational and perceptive modeling, that goes beyond text generation alone. That is why, I prefer to work across multiple domains and combine them, specifically pushing the state of the art through research and publications, while also engineering end-to-end, production-ready AI solutions that bridge the gap between theory and scalable products.

Currently, I work at Pleia AI, where I engineer generative and agentic systems that orchestrate LLM tools with multimodal reasoning for personalized healthcare. Previously, as a Research Scientist at AstraZeneca, I developed novel self-supervised learning paradigms for the investigation of EGFR and CMET effect on cancer progression.
