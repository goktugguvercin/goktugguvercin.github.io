Philosophy & Approach

I am a Machine Learning Scientist specializing in Language Modeling, Agentic AI, and Computer Vision. I view these fields not as separate disciplines, but as integral, interconnected components of intelligence to build cognitive thinking systems.

From a human perspective, the world is sensed and interpreted through sight, sound, and interaction. We then reason through this multimodal experience, draw conclusions, and compress it into language to communicate.

That is why I prefer to work across multiple domains and combine them, specifically pushing the state of the art through research and publications, while also engineering end-to-end, production-ready AI solutions.
